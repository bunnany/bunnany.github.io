ABOUT DO-OKIES

Do you love ice cream, cookies and donuts but don’t have enough money for all. Well we thought of an idea that can satisfy your taste buds and save you money all at the same time, introducing the Do-okie!!

A do-okie is ice cream sandwiched between 2 chocolate chip cookies with a hole in the middle to give them the donut look and, with sprinkles stuck around the ice cream

USP + Target Market

do-okies are the perfect combination of two classic treats. With a fluffy donut looking exterior and a creamy ice cream filling, they’re a fun and unique way to satisfy your sweet tooth. Our target market is thordon students as the ice cream and cookies combo are young kids go to option. We are making our theme bright colors that also attract younger kids. So that is why we think that this food product is most suitable for the Thorndon students

Pricing

We are planning on selling each do-okies for $3.

Packaging

For our packaging we thought little boxes would be the best option especially since we have ice cream so that you can hold them upright. They are hygienic and environmentally friendly since you can recycle them after use, they are also waterproof. We liked the idea of saying thank you so we’ve come up with the idea of having little waterproof stickers that just say thank you.
