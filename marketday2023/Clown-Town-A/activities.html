Clown Town

Spin the Clown - 50c for a Spin

Our first game is “Spin the Clown” where we have a wheel with the picture of a clown on it, and different options. (eg: a bag of fun coloured popcorn, a cake pop, or even just nothing)

Down the Clown - $1 for 2 Tries

Our second game is “Down the Clown”, where plastic cups will be set up in a triangle with the image of a clown spread out through them. There will be a point system in place for what they hit. (eg: head=ten points meaning you get a bag of popcorn)

Feed the Clown - $1 for 3 Tries

Our last game is “Feed the clown” where contestants will have 3 chances to score an M&M into one of three cups. These cups will be shaped to look like clown mouths.
