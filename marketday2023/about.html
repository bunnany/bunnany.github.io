History

Year 10 Market Day is an annual tradition at WGC. We are not entirely sure when it started, but we estimate almost 30 years since the first Market Day. Until 2022, Market Day was held on the quad and in the hall, however in 2022 we moved to the Brook courts. Previously Market Day was for WGC students only, however to expand the target market, Thorndon School staff and students are invited. Process

Year 10 Economics and Enterprise students put together a Business Plan to create a completely new brand. Before teams can go ahead, they must face up to the "Dragon's Den". Here the teams pitch their idea and aim for a green light. Then they advertise and create their product, and finally sell it at Market Day.
