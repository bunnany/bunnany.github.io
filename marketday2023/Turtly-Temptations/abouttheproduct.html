About our product

Our brand sells turtle-themed sweet scrolls with custom toppings. Our products are original recipes made from a yeast based dough with homemade fillings. These turtle scrolls cannot be found in your average supermarket or bakery but a variety of them can be found at our stall on market day. We will have four types of scroll with a set amount of filling in each but the customer can have us add more toppings for a set price.
