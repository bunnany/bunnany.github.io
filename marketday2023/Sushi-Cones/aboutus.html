Sushi Cones

About Us

Sushi cones are a deconstructed version of sushi, taking the sushi shape that we all know and love to a more practical cone shape. They are easy to eat and portable for all occasions. Comes in two different flavours costing either $3 or $4.

We will make sure the project is hygienic by following the instructions on how to properly prepare the food plus we will sanitise things that need to be.
