Frequently asked  questions

What is your curry like?

Our curry is a tasty potato curry made with fresh Indian spices.

Is the naan and curry vegetarian?

Yes, both our naan and curry is vegetarian, made homemade, with fresh ingredients.

What do I get when I buy a naan and curry?

You will get average sized oval-shaped naan, with some scoops of curry in the middle.

How much is your naan?

Our naan alone costs $3, and curry is an extra $1.
