OUR PRODUCT

WHAT IS IT

Our product is a burger slider that is inspired by international cuisines. It is made up of a soft dinner roll, a juicy mince patty, a slice of cheese and your choice of sauce. These choices include, tomato sauce, mayonaise, periperi and gravy. You also have the option to add extra sauces from the list to spice up your sliders!

COSTS

Our cost options are as follows: $4 for 1 slider or $7 for 2 sliders.

ALLERGENS

Our product contains gluten, dairy, meat and eggs. It may contain nuts.
