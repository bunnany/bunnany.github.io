OUR PRODUCT

Our product is a burger slider that is inspired by international cuisines. It is made up of a soft dinner roll, a juicy mince patty, a slice of cheese and your choice of sauce. These choices include, tomato, mayonaise, periperi and gravy. You also have the option to add extra sauces from the list to spice up your sliders!
