Product

What is our product?

Hey everyone, we are Imaan, Katelyn, Abi and Abhi!! Over the last term, we have worked really hard to come up with an idea we think you all will love! Our product is . . . COOKIE FRIES!!!

We have two delicious flavours; Double Choc (chocolate cookie with chocolate chips), and Funky Fiesta (sugar cookie with rainbow sprinkles). We also have two yummy sauce flavours to choose from; ketchup (red coloured vanilla icing), and barbeque (chocolate icing).

$2.99 buys you a serving with a cookie flavour of your choice, and a sauce of your choice. For just one dollar more ($3.99), you can buy our loaded fries!! This comes with your choice of cookie flavour and sauce, with whipped cream and popping candy!! Each serving comes with 3 delicious cookies for a lovely snack.
