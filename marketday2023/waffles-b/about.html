Who its made for

This product is advertised mainly for Wellington girls college students, for market day in term 3.

The main idea

Waffle express is a waffle on a stick to give easy and mess free waffle. As we all know nobody wants sticky hands! The train idea is so that you can stop at stations eg: sauce and toppings are all going to each be a station that you stop at as if you are on a train. We choose this idea because we want to let the customers options to choose what they desire instead of having pre selected flavours.
