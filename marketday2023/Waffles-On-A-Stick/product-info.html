Waffle Express

Waffle Express is a waffle on a stick giving mess free, easy to eat waffles. (No one wants sticky hands when trying to enjoy their breakfast.) Stop at different stations to build your waffle delights such as, Sauceville and Topping Lane. We chose these stations with our customers in mind, giving them the freedom to customize.

All Aboard

This staion is where you will be deciding on whether you want savoury or sweet. Choose how many waffles you'd like to purchase, taking in mind our deal of 2 for $3 or 3 for $4. This is also where you will be paying.

North Pole

Here, you will get your delicious vanilla icecream.

Sauceville

Choose from our range of sauces, caramel, chocolate and maple syrup. Don't add too much though, or your waffle will sog!

Topping Lane

Sweet toppings for your sweet tooth. Choose from crushed cookies, rainbow rock chunks and white chocolate.
