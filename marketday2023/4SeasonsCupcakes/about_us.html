ABOUT US

Our mission is to supply our customers with a quality product that displays creativity and helps our business thrive!

Our main three goals are:

  • Keep costs as low as possible by researching available ingredients and making wise choices.
  • Break even and make a profit by managing our costs along the way and ensuring we will not need to sell many cupcakes before breaking even.
  • Produce a well-presented, quality product by crafting with care and attention.
