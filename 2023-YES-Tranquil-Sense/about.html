Our Brand

Tranquil Sense is a small business founded under the Young Enterprise Scheme (YES for short) which is a business program where high schoolers start up and run their own business. The aim of Tranquil Sense as a brand revolves around combating the issue of sleep deprevation in teenagers, a common occurence in our daily lives. Tranquil Sense is brought to you by three young entrpreneurs from Wellington Girls College - aiming to improve your daily sense, with our sense.

Our Product

Our product addresses this issue using cosmetic aromatherapy by utilizing the fragrance and skincare properties of Lavender and Rosemary essential oils. The scent of lavender has long been proven to help increase sleep quality and quantity, whereas rosemary is a very energising scent and has the opposite effect - helpling to improve memory and cognitive ability.
